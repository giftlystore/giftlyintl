Help & FAQs

How can I track my delivery?
After sending for delivery, a tracking number will be generated. Copy the tracking info using the "Copy Tracking Info" button and provide it to support to check your delivery status.
How do I place a gift order?
To place an order, select a product, choose the quantity, optionally add a note and sender name, then click "Send for Delivery". You will receive a tracking number which you can copy and use to follow up with support.
What if I need to cancel my order?
Contact support immediately with your tracking number. Cancellation depends on the order status and delivery process.
Can I add a personalized note?
Yes! There is a "Printed Note" field in the order form where you can add a short message for the recipient.
